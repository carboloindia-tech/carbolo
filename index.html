01 — The leak

Where dealerships bleed money.

Three places. All preventable.

01↓

Lead response is broken.

Average WhatsApp and web reply takes two to six hours. By then the buyer has talked to three competitors. Roughly seventy percent of leads die in the first forty-eight.

We reply in under thirty seconds. Across every channel. Sunday at 11pm is just another shift.

02↓

Salesperson quality is wildly inconsistent.

A new hire can't explain variant differences. Hallucinates features. Folds the moment they hear "Tata safer hai" or "EMI zyada hai". Top performers cannot be cloned.

One trained brain, fluent across every variant, every objection, every regional script — Hindi, English, Hinglish. It never forgets, and when uncertain, it escalates.

03↓

The showroom dies on Sundays.

Customer walks in. No salesperson is free. They sit in the car alone, leave confused, never return. The most expensive square footage in retail, going to waste.

Every parked car becomes an interactive product experience. Voice-driven. Animated. Variant-locked. The Apple Store moment, in a Hyundai showroom.
